Children's Sabbath

At Chikonohono Seventh-Day Adventist Church, we believe in nurturing the future generation in their faith journey. Our Children's Sabbath program provides a fun and engaging environment where children can learn about God's love, the Bible, and Christian values. Through interactive activities, songs, stories, and age-appropriate lessons, children can develop a strong foundation in their faith. We invite you to join us for Children's Sabbath and see how your child can grow closer to God!

Global Mission Evangelism

Spreading Love Through Service: The Dorcas Our commitment to Global Mission Evangelism extends beyond sharing the gospel message. The Dorcas department embodies this spirit by offering practical acts of love and care to those in need, both within our community and around the world. Through the Dorcas ministry, our church family works together to provide clothing, food, and other forms of assistance to those facing hardship. Story of Hope

At Chikonohono Seventh-Day Adventist Church, music plays a vital role in sharing our story of hope. Through uplifting hymns, soul-stirring choruses, and inspiring praise music, our worship services come alive with the joy of faith.
